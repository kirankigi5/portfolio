I am a Software Engineer currently pursuing my Master’s in Computer Science at [New York University's Courant Institute](https://cims.nyu.edu/dynamic/), where my focus lies at the intersection of traditional systems engineering and artificial intelligence. Prior to this, I completed my B.Tech in Computer Science at the [Indian Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad](https://www.iiit.ac.in/).

In terms of my engineering experience, I am deeply interested in **Agentic Engineering**, the architectural discipline required to turn raw, unpredictable LLMs into reliable, production-ready software systems. I began my journey as a Student Researcher at the [Robotics Research Center](https://rocon.in/people), engineering asynchronous telemetry pipelines and low-latency Kalman filtering algorithms in C++, and subsequently worked as a Software Engineer at Ambitio. More recently, I have focused on architecting complex distributed systems, including a Local-First AI Inference Gateway and a sharded transactional database leveraging Multi-Paxos.

Beyond my academic and professional work, I am an avid builder with a passion for open-source software and the rapidly evolving landscape of local AI models. As a curious engineer, I am driven to connect complex architectural hypotheses and test them in practice. Generally, it deepens my understanding of highly available systems and drives my pursuit of engineering robust, fault-tolerant infrastructure.
