LocalSketch is a collaborative whiteboard built around the principles of local-first software and peer-to-peer synchronization. Users can create or join a room (e.g., `daily-standup`) and instantly sketch together in real-time, completely bypassing traditional central server dependencies.

By leveraging peer-to-peer networking protocols, LocalSketch ensures that users own their data and that the application functions seamlessly offline or on local networks without sacrificing collaborative features.

[View the live app here](https://localsketch.vercel.app/)
